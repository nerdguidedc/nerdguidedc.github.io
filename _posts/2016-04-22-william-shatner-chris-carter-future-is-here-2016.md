Buy tickets to attend an evening with William Shatner and Chris Carter.
William "Call me Bill” Shatner will be talking for over an hour about
Star Trek, his life and work on the 50th anniversary of the legendary
TV series. You’ll hear an in-depth interview with Chris Carter, the
creator of The X-Files, who will tell stories about the series and its
triumphant return this season. You’ll also meet the stars of SyFy’s smash
hit 12 Monkeys.

Use the discount code "NERDNITE25" for 50% of the ticket price. Tickets
can be [purchased online](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/smithsonian-magazines-2016-the-future-is-here-festivaltm-registration-21495104436)
under the "Star Fleet Captain" Level. You can also attend the free
Smithsonian Magazine *The Future Is Here Festival* on Saturday and Sunday.
